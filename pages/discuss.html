To stay up to date with us, you can follow our blog or the Mozilla Science Lab blog. You can also talk to us on IRC, but most conversation happens on these mailing lists:

announcements (archives) Announcements about upcoming bootcamps and other events. 3-5 messages/month (broadcast only).
discuss (archives) The main channel for discussion of direction and technology. 5-20 messages/week
instructors (archives) Advertisements for bootcamp instructors and helpers. 1-2 messages/month
